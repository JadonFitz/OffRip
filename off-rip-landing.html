The Film

Write what you know.

A high schooler trains for the skimboarding tournament that could save his family's house — while the world he grew up in pulls in every direction at once.

Shot in St. Petersburg, Florida — the town writer-director-star Jadon Cal grew up in. Off Rip is the first feature film ever made about skimboarding. Pro skimmers including Red Bull athlete Lucas Fink appear in the film, with boards by Zap Skimboards out of Venice, FL.

Hands down the most heartfelt journey I've seen in a while, with sick skimboarding footage that has never been seen with such vibrant colors. — IMDb · 9.3/10

Funny. Exciting. Specific to the salt-air specificity of Floridian beach culture — and universal in the family at its core.
